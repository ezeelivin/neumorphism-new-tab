Privacy Policy for Neumorphism New Tab

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled when you use our Neumorphism New Tab extension.

Information We Don't Collect

Neumorphism New Tab is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect:

  • Personally identifiable information
  • Health information
  • Financial and payment information
  • Authentication information
  • Personal communications
  • Location data
  • Web history
  • User activity
  • Website content

Local Storage

The extension only stores your preferences and settings locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device and is only used to remember your customization choices for the new tab page.

Bookmark Access

Neumorphism New Tab accesses your bookmarks to display them in the new tab interface. These bookmarks remain on your device and are accessed through Chrome's bookmarks API. We do not store or transmit your bookmark data.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking services, or external APIs that might collect user data.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the extension.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
